BAGBAG

Established in 2004, BAGBAG Manufacturing Ltd. is a top promotional bags manufacturer based in Xiamen, China. Our focus is to build long-term partnerships with our customers and bring profitable advancement through cost effective manufacturing and cutting edge product development.

We have successfully delivered millions upon millions of promotional bags to the USA and the EU, from cooler bags, tote bags, nonwoven bags and drawstring bags, to backpacks, computer bags, and the quantity goes on and on. With our competitive prices and variety of custom products, we produce and design bags for several promotional companies of the ASI TOP 40 SUPPLIERS in the USA.

As you must have noticed, more and more companies that claim to specialize in bags make and popping up at industry trade shows,Alibaba.com, GLOBALSOURCES and other B2B websites. Here are just some of ways we stand out from those other companies:

1), Best Price: One of our factory investors is fabric manufacturer, our price is very aggressive and competitive. No matter what your budget is, we have a solution for you.

2), Fast Delivery: Most of the orders can be finished in 30 – 35 days, for some rush orders we can make in 20 days.

3), Creative and Development: We have our own staff of bags designers, engineers and operations managers, who have expertise in multiple bags categories. Every year we introduce more than 200 new styles, so our customers always have new bags in their line to attract their clients.

4), We keep a full eye on Quality Control during material purchasing, production, printing, packaging and shipping. We offer quality inspection reports, we do QC for every bag for every order we do.

5), Our factory BAGBAG bags manufacturing has passed the third party BV's factory audit to ensure you that we meet responsible quality and social compliance standards. Many factories are not registered in China, so they work with (and are at the mercy of) trading companies and/or agents

Our quality and service combined with our low prices makes all Obaili bags outstanding values
